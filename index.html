Intel's Sustainability Journey

Building a better tomorrow through innovation and responsibility

1970s: The Beginning

Intel is founded and begins its journey in semiconductor innovation, laying the groundwork for future sustainable technology.

1980s: Energy Efficiency Focus

Intel begins developing more power-efficient chips, focusing on performance and environmental impact.

1990s: Green Infrastructure

Intel starts investing in green building design, pioneering eco-friendly corporate campuses.

2000s: Sustainability Reporting

Intel begins publishing corporate responsibility reports, tracking environmental goals transparently.

2010s: Emission Reductions

Intel invests heavily in emission reduction technology and sustainable supply chains.

2022: 100% Renewable Energy

Intel reached 100% renewable electricity use in U.S., Europe, Israel, and Malaysia operations.

2023: Water Positive

Intel restored more water than it consumed in its global operations for the first time.

2024: Zero Waste to Landfill

Major Intel campuses achieved zero hazardous waste to landfill certification.

2030: Carbon Neutral

Intel aims to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across its operations by 2030.
